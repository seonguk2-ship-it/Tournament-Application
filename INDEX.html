4. Booking and Cancellation Policies

1. Course Reservation Confirmation & Deposit

Course booking is confirmed only after a **50% deposit** of the total estimated tournament cost is received. The booking is officially reserved upon deposit completion.

2. Cancellation Penalty (Cancellation Fee)

For cancellations made **1 month (30 days) prior** to the event date, a **100% penalty of the total estimated cost** will be incurred.

3. Large Tournament Exclusivity Clause (80+ people)

For tournaments with 80 or more participants, we (the Golf Course) guarantee that **no other large tournament of similar size** will be booked **three weeks before or three weeks after (total 6 weeks)** the confirmed event date, to support the success of your event.
